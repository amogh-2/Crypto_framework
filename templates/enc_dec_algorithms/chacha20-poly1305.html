ChaCha20-Poly1305 is a high-speed, authenticated encryption algorithm that ensures both data confidentiality and integrity. It combines the ChaCha20 stream cipher for encryption with the Poly1305 authentication function to detect tampering. Using a 256-bit key and a unique nonce, it provides fast, secure, and efficient encryption, even on low-power devices. Widely used in TLS, VPNs, and secure messaging, it is preferred for its resistance to side-channel attacks and consistent performance across hardware and software.
